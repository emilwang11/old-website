About Me

My dream job as a child was to be an inventor. I had no idea what this entailed other than creating solutions for peoples problems. My passion for technology and innovation has became more clear as I got older, leading me to pursue a degree in Systems Design engineering at Waterloo.

With an interest in software engineering, weekends are frequently spent at hackathons or working on side projects. I also love being involved and developing student leadership. So far at Waterloo I have been part of Waterloo's Hyperloop team (Waterloop), an Engineering Ambassador and an organizer for Hack the North. In my free time, I love to go on food adventures, read and discover new music. I'm also a huge beach and ocean lover (teal color of the site inspired by the color of the water).

Being highly motivated and diligent, I am constantly learning and taking new opportunities to push my limits on what I can achieve.
