About Me

My dream job as a child was to be an inventor. I had no idea what that entailed other than creating solutions for people's problems. I kept a book full of ideas for inventions/innovations from apps to newly designed backpacks. My pull towards innovation evolved into a fasicnation for technology, leading me to pursue a degree in Systems Design engineering at Waterloo.

With an interest in software engineering, I'm often at hackathons or trying to learn new languages. I'm currently a software developer at TD Labs. I also love being involved in the school and community. I'm currently an organizer for Hack the North, Canada's largest hackathon, an Engineering Ambassador and was on Waterloo's Hyperloop team (Waterloop). In my free time, I love to go on food adventures, read and discover new music. I also love the ocean, dogs and the color teal!

Being highly motivated and diligent, I am constantly learning and taking new opportunities to push my limits on what I can achieve.
